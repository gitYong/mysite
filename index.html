My name is Yongyi Zhao (赵永一). I am a PhD student in the Rice University Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) department, working with Professor Ashok Veeraraghavan in the Computational Imaging Group. From 2021-2023, I was supported by a 2-year National Library of Medicine (NLM) fellowship. I completed my bachelor's degree at Carnegie Mellon University, also majoring in ECE. My topic of interest is signal processing, specifically focusing on computational imaging. I also enjoy studying digital logic design and software systems.
