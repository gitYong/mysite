Violin

Lot of scales, vibrato'ing took a while.

I first started playing violin in 2nd grade. I have played solo, in quartets, and full orchestras. I was trained under the Suzuki method by Sanyi Lin. I completed the first 6 books of the Suzuki repetoire before proceeding to more advanced pieces. My favorite piece to perform is Monti Czardas.
